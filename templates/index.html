About Us

Who are we?

We are two friends who have been hosting the quiz together at Wirströms Pub for nearly 10 years. The quiz started with three quizmasters but we have been two for the past few years.

What do we do?

We host a quiz with a unique format that is adjusted to suit the participants, the occasion and the time requirements. The quiz can be booked to be hosted at Wirströms Pub and depending on the amount of participants, either the whole of or a part section of the basement can be reserved. Around 100 people can be seated at a maximum and the quizzes we host regularly see all tables booked out. We have also hosted quizzes away from the pub, including at a local community meeting and also for a function evening for a multinational company at a local retreat.
