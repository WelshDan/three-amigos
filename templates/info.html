About Us

Who are we?

We are two friends who have been hosting the quiz together at Wirströms Pub for nearly 10 years. The quiz started with three quizmasters but we have been two for the past few years.

What do we do?

We host a quiz with a unique format that is adjusted to suit the participants, the occasion and the time requirements.

Where do we do it?

We host quizzes regularly at Wirströms Pub in Gamla Stan (Stockholm) - See dates and results for more information.

Private quizzes can be booked to be hosted at Wirströms Pub where around 100 people can be seated at a maximum and depending on the amount of participants, either the whole of or a part section of the basement can be reserved.

We have also hosted quizzes away from the pub, including at a local community meeting and for a function evening for a multinational company at a local retreat.

Fill in an enquiry form to check availability or a booking form to book a quiz.
