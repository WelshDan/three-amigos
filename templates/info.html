Who are The Three Amigos

The quiz started with three quizmasters (hence the name) but we have been two for several years now. There have been a few guest quizmasters over the years too.

How long have you hosted the quiz at Wirströms Pub?

We have been hosting the quiz for more than 11 years. We have hosted more than 200 quizzes during that time.

How do we attend a quiz at Wirströms Pub?

We host quizzes every second Thursday that start at 19.00. The maximum number of players per team is 6. Tables can be booked via Wirströms Pub by emailing booking@wirstromspub.se

Wirströms Pub in Gamla Stan (Stockholm) - See dates and results for more information.

What shall I do if I am interested in hiring you for a quiz?

You can fill in a booking form if you know what you want and when you want it. Or you can fill in an enquiry form and we can see if we can arrange what you want together.

Can we order a quiz that we can host ourselves?

Sure! Send an email to threeamigosquiz@gmail.com and we can discuss it

What is the quiz layout?

The quiz is set up in 2 halves with each half being divided up into 4 rounds. A total of 8 rounds per quiz. Each round contains 5 questions - 4 standard quiz questions and 1 audio question. Each half also contains a "Mystery Thing" which connects to the answers of some questions.

How hard are the questions?

There are a variety of difficulty levels to the questions. We suggest teams of 4-6 people to cover the range of subjects but of course smaller teams can enjoy the quiz too.

How much does the quiz cost?

The quiz at Wirströms Pub is free to enter - just don't forget to email your table booking.

What can be won at the quiz?

First prize: Trophy + Wirströms Pub vouchers Second prize: Trophy + Wirströms Pub vouchers Third prize: Wirströms Pub vouchers Wheel prizes: Gifts from our sponsors at Comics Heaven

About Us

Who are we?

We are two friends who have been hosting the quiz together at Wirströms Pub for nearly 10 years. The quiz started with three quizmasters but we have been two for the past few years.

What do we do?

We host a quiz with a unique format that is adjusted to suit the participants, the occasion and the time requirements.

Where do we do it?

We host quizzes regularly at Wirströms Pub in Gamla Stan (Stockholm) - See dates and results for more information.

Private quizzes can be booked to be hosted at Wirströms Pub where around 100 people can be seated at a maximum and depending on the amount of participants, either the whole of or a part section of the basement can be reserved.

We have also hosted quizzes away from the pub, including at a local community meeting and for a function evening for a multinational company at a local retreat.

Fill in an enquiry form to check availability or a booking form to book a quiz.
